ASIC Miner Configuration

This is the basic guide on how to set up your ASIC miner for Kaspa mining.

Getting Started

To begin mining Kaspa with your ASIC miner, follow these steps:

  • Kaspa Wallet address: Web Wallet
  • ASIC miner compatible with the Kaspa hashing algorithm
  • Access to a power source and internet connection for your ASIC miner

Kaspa Wallet Address

A wallet address is required to receive payouts for your mined shares on this pool server. Once the total mined value surpasses the payout threshold, your coins will be sent to this wallet address.

ASIC Miner Configuration

To mine Kaspa with your ASIC miner, ensure it is configured with the following parameters:

  • POOL STRATUM ADDRESS AND PORT - stratum+tcp://fr.{{ stratumAddress }}
  • YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS - Your valid Kaspa wallet address
  • PASSWORD - Use 'x' or leave it blank

Example ASIC Miner Configuration

Here's an example of how you can configure your ASIC miner for Kaspa mining:

cgminer -o stratum+tcp://fr.{{ stratumAddress }} -u YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS -p x

Please replace YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS with your actual Kaspa wallet address.
